# 🍽️ SnapCal

> Snap a photo. Get nutrition insights. Track your health.

SnapCal is an AI-powered food tracking application that helps users monitor calories and nutrition with a simple photo of their meal.

Instead of manually searching foods, estimating portions, and entering nutritional data, users can simply take a picture and let AI handle the analysis.

Built with a photography-first experience, SnapCal focuses on speed, simplicity, and consistency.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📸 AI Food Scanning

* Capture meals using the built-in camera
* AI identifies food items from images
* Automatic nutrition estimation

### 🎯 Personalized Nutrition Goals

* Daily calorie targets
* Protein goals
* Carbohydrate goals
* Fat goals

Generated automatically during onboarding based on:

* Age
* Gender
* Height
* Weight
* Activity level
* Health goals

### 🍱 Meal Tracking

* Save scanned meals
* View meal history
* Organized by date
* Detailed nutrition breakdown

### 📊 Progress Analytics

* Daily nutrition tracking
* Weekly trends
* Monthly insights
* Macro tracking

### 👤 User Profile

* Personal information management
* Goal customization
* App preferences
* Theme settings

## 🚀 How It Works

1. Complete onboarding
2. Set your health goal
3. Take a photo of your meal
4. AI analyzes the food
5. Review nutrition estimates
6. Save the meal
7. Track your progress
